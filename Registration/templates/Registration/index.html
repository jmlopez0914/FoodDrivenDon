Our Vision

    To provide a fast and efficient mode of transportation for donated goods to food banks around the Northwest Indiana region. We want to be able to keep local food banks stocked with goods in order to effectively aid in ending food insecurity in Northwest Indiana.

How FoodDriven Works

    FoodDriven is a non-profit organization that makes it convenient to donate goods. The base of the organization begins with the "Driven" part of the name. We receive addresses and times for pickup through our registration page (linked in the navigation bar) and send out designated drivers to receive your donations. The donations go through inspection and are then dropped off at the local Food Bank. Although food is emphasized in the organization's name, we accept unexpired, non-perishable food, new/lightly used clothing items, new hygiene products, and toys/games.

Why Choose FoodDriven

    FoodDriven is a 21st century adaptation to the donation and collection of goods. Just like Uber and GrubHub, FoodDriven is a convenient way to take on a task. Instead of coming around once a year like a postal service, we manage our time with the simple submission of a form. Once a form is submitted, the goods will be picked up from your doorstep. There is no driving, paying, or really anything but preparing your goods for the doorstep. This free, easy-to-use service has great potential in bringing many participants to donate goods.
