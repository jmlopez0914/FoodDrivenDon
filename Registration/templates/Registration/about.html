About FoodDriven



Registration Process


    The first thing to the registration process is to understand what you are registering for. The registration page is a page to let FoodDriven know that you have goods to be picked up. You can register by clicking the 'register' tab on the top of the webpage. The registration includes: name, email, home address, city, state, and pickup-date. This information will let the drivers of FoodDriven know where and when donations are being picked up. Any registrant should put the pickup-date for at least 2 days in advance to help FoodDriven organize pickup trips. You will get an e-mail confirmation that your registration has been received.

Location


    FoodDriven is located in Northwest Indiana. Due to restrictions, most likely any registrant outside the Northwest Indiana region will be sadly refused the service until expansion of the organization takes place.
